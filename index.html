TetraDefense

Play

How to play

1. The red curve shows the path that enemies will take
2. Click to place towers. The blue timer at the top right shows cooldown until you can place again
3. Press spacebar to rotate the tetrad before placing it (may not work on your first tetrad)
4. If you place 4 tetrads in a pattern to make a larger tetrad, it will create a superior mega tetrad.
